Wilks Calculator


Wilks =

  • The Wilks Formula is used to measure the strength of a powerlifter against other powerlifters regardless of different weight classes.
  • As a rough estimate:
  • 1-300: Beginner
  • 300-375: Intermediate
  • 375-450: Advanced
  • 450+: Elite

TDEE Calculator


TDEE =

  • TDEE stands for Total Dailey Energy Expenditure, or the amount of calories you burn in a day.
  • Add/Subtract calories from your TDEE in order to gain/lose weight
